CropSight - Crop Disease Detection System

CropSight is an agricultural support system that detects crop diseases from leaf images and provides farmers with actionable recommendations to protect crop health and reduce yield loss.

Leaf Image Based Detection Users upload crop leaf images through a simple web interface for fast disease screening.
Disease Type + Confidence The deep learning model identifies healthy or diseased crops and returns confidence score.
Treatment Recommendations Detected conditions are mapped to pesticide, organic, and field-practice suggestions.

CropSight Project Overview

The application preprocesses uploaded leaf images and passes them through a trained computer-vision model to classify crop condition, identify disease symptoms, and provide confidence-backed output.

Disease Detection Workflow

Image upload, preprocessing, deep learning inference, and result display are connected in a single web flow.

Recommendation Engine

Based on disease type, the system generates treatment suggestions including pesticides, organic options, and field management practices.

Sustainable Farming Impact

Early disease detection and preventive advisories help farmers take timely action and reduce crop loss.

Detailed Project Description

The architecture includes an image upload interface, preprocessing module, machine learning classifier, result visualization dashboard, and recommendation engine for practical agricultural support.

System Scope and Objective (Editable)

CropSight combines computer vision, machine learning, and agricultural domain knowledge to identify crop diseases from leaf images and support informed decisions in the field.

Prediction and Treatment Flow (Editable)

After classifying the crop as healthy or diseased, the system provides confidence score and disease-specific treatment guidance to improve response quality.

Weather Alert and Crop Precautions (Editable)

The system can integrate weather forecast data to provide preventive guidance before disease outbreaks or environmental damage occurs.

Example Advisory Output (Editable)

  • High Humidity / Rain Expected: Increase field ventilation, avoid over-watering, and apply preventive fungicide spray to reduce fungal infections.
  • High Temperature Forecast: Ensure proper irrigation during early morning or evening, provide shade protection for sensitive crops, and monitor leaf moisture stress.
  • Heavy Rainfall Warning: Improve drainage channels, avoid fertilizer application before rain, and inspect crops for early signs of root rot.
  • Strong Wind Conditions: Secure young plants with support structures and avoid pesticide spraying during high wind periods.
  • Cold Weather / Temperature Drop: Use protective crop covers, delay irrigation during cold mornings, and monitor plants for frost damage.

This feature supports preventive action before disease spread and helps improve sustainable farming outcomes.
