VitaWatch - AI Health Monitoring System

VitaWatch monitors live health signals, identifies risk patterns with AI, and generates actionable alerts for early intervention and better patient safety.

Real-Time Vitals Tracking Collects heart rate, SpO2, temperature, and activity signals through an easy monitoring interface.
AI Risk Scoring Uses machine learning to classify health status and highlight abnormal trends with confidence score.
Alert and Guidance Generates practical recommendations and escalation alerts for patients, caretakers, and medical staff.

VitaWatch Project Overview

The application ingests continuous health data, preprocesses it, and applies AI models to detect anomalies, classify risk level, and support timely preventive action.

Signal Processing Pipeline

Sensor data is cleaned, normalized, and structured before inference to improve prediction quality.

Alert and Escalation Engine

When abnormal values are detected, the system sends alerts with severity context and next-step guidance.

Healthcare Impact

Early risk detection helps reduce emergency events and supports proactive, data-driven care decisions.

Detailed Project Description

The architecture includes data capture interface, preprocessing module, AI health classifier, trend visualization dashboard, and recommendation engine for actionable care support.

System Scope and Objective (Editable)

VitaWatch combines IoT-enabled sensing, machine learning, and clinical thresholds to monitor health in real time and identify early warning signs.

Prediction and Alert Flow (Editable)

After processing live vitals, the model classifies health condition as normal, moderate risk, or high risk and generates advisory recommendations.

Risk Alert and Preventive Recommendations (Editable)

The system can integrate upcoming health trends and threshold rules to provide preventive guidance before critical conditions develop.

Example Advisory Output (Editable)

  • High Heart Rate Alert: Suggest immediate rest, hydration, and repeat check after a short interval; escalate if persistent.
  • Low SpO2 Warning: Recommend breathing support steps, reduce physical activity, and contact medical support if values stay low.
  • Fever Trend Rising: Prompt temperature logging, hydration plan, and consultation advice if increase continues.
  • Irregular Sleep + Stress Pattern: Suggest recovery routine, lower stimulant intake, and monitor next-day symptom patterns.
  • Critical Combined Risk: Trigger priority alert workflow to caretakers or hospital contact points with summarized report.

This feature enables preventive intervention before severe health deterioration and improves long-term wellness outcomes.
